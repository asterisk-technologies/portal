Privacy Policy

At Asterisk, we are committed to keeping your personal information private and safe so that no harm comes to you. This private policy is going to be talking about the ways we collect and use your information, safeguard, and disclose your information when you use our website.

We also collect all information entered into the job application. Any payment transactions that are made through our website will also be collected. Other data usage such as IP address, browsing activity and browser type may also be collected.

How we will use your information:

Information sharing and disclosure

We may need to share your information with our partners or third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, so that we can get the things you need. We also disclose your information in response to legal requests or to protect the rights of property and safety.

Data Security

We want to protect your information and will reach those security measures to protect your personal information from any altercations, destruction, disclosure, or any unauthorized access. However, we cannot always guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We will keep a hold of personal information for our the purposes listed above. The only way the privacy policy may not be followed is if it is not required or permitted by the law.

Your Rights and your Choices

You have access to delete and update any personal information. Please contact us if you have any questions about your privacy.

Changes to this privacy policy

We hold the right to upgrade or modify this private policy at any given time. We will be notifying you if any privacy policy changes are made, so please look out for the updates posted on our website.
